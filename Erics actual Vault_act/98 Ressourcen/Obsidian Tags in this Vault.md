# **Obsidian Tags in this Vault**
## **2022-07-26 10:06**
___
The following Tags are used:

- %*# pkm*% for permanent notes
	- %*#concept*% for concepts and thoughts! Concept are clear and precise and can easily addressed with an exclamation point.
	- %*# idea*% for ideas. Ideas arrive as new thoughts based on various information connected to each other. Ideas are not bullet-proof concepts and can be quite often addressed with question mark.
