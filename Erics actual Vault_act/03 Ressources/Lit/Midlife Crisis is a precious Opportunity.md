# Midlife Crisis is a valuable Opportunity
## 2023-03-27 14:31
___
# Note Section
## Notes
Contrary to popular belief, a mid-life crisis is a precious opportunity. It’s a rare moment when you are forced to question your basic assumptions, which can open the door to making profoundly positive changes in your life.

Tiago Forte in: ([Tiago’s 2022 Annual Review: From Mid-Life Crisis to Reinvention (fortelabs.com)](https://fortelabs.com/blog/tiagos-2022-annual-review-from-mid-life-crisis-to-reinvention/))
