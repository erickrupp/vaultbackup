# **Dezimalstellen in Notion**
## **2022-02-26 20:43**

for decimals in formulae:

1. multiply the entire formula, after you have established it with 100
2. surround this formula with function 'round' - Don't forget the additional brackets around the entire new formula (with 100)
3. divide the entire 'new' formula by 100
4. The number you use depends on the number of decimals. the number of decimals determine the number of zeros after the 1
